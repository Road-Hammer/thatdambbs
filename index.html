That Damn BBS

_

Who the Hell Are We?

Old-school. Gen X. Veteran trucker. Grew up when dial-up was king and Wildcat was gospel. That Damn BBS is my way of bringing that world back—raw, unpolished, and real.

What Is This?

This isn't a blog. It’s not a podcast. It’s a digital BBS reboot for the modern era—hosted on GitHub, powered by grit, fueled by late nights and diesel fumes.

We’re building a community space for truckers, technicians, coders, misfits, preppers, and anyone else who knows the value of doing things the hard way—because it’s the right way.

Where’s It Going?

Eventually: full BBS-style features. Self-hosted tools. Internal file shares. Secure vault access. Integrated AI for roadside diagnostics, payroll audits, trip logging, and even community message boards.

Right now? We’re barely past the "hello world" stage. But it’s real. And it’s growing.

Why Should You Care?

Because everything today feels plastic. Sanitized. Disposable. This ain’t that. This is a call back to what tech used to be—personal, weird, rebellious, useful.

I need help. My Step-son Tyler is struggling. His friend Billy is AWOL. I’m juggling a dozen differnt projects and Trucking OTR. But this matters. And if you’ve ever heard the sound of a 56k modem and felt that itch in your soul… maybe it matters to you, too.

Want In?

If you’ve got time, talent, or just want to see this thing live and breathe, fork the repo or drop me a message. I’ll take coders, designers, testers, logistics freaks, or anyone who can keep their word and bring some fire.
