Road Hawk

Where Diesel Meets Data

Road Hawk is a frontline tool built by and for truckers—designed to take the mystery (and bullshit) out of payroll discrepancies, missed reimbursements, and shady log edits.

  • 🔍 Forensic trip logging
  • 📜 Payroll auditing with cross-verified logs
  • 📱 Syncs across mobile and desktop (eventually)
  • 🔐 Built with privacy, not platform tracking

This project is still in early development. Expect raw edges, but know it’s being built with grit, sweat, and first-hand knowledge from the highway frontlines.
