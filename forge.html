What Is The Forge?

This is the think tank, the mad science lab, the R&D pit stop for That Damn BBS and everything running under the Road Hammer banner. When an idea hits at 3:13 AM, it gets hammered out right here.

Currently In Development

  • Road Hawk: Forensic payroll auditing + trip tracking software built by and for truckers.
  • Custom AI Server Stack: Hybrid Windows/Ubuntu dev box running LAN-based AI on a shoestring budget.
  • Wildcat Retro Web Framework: HTML/CSS templates for that old-school ANSI magic.
  • Intelligence Vault: Operational research, project management files, and live link archive.

Experimental Labs

  • Prepping tech integrations (solar, wind, satellite)
  • Automated dispatch mapping
  • AI-forensics for fuel cards and logs
  • Legacy hardware resurrection & repurposing

Want to Contribute?

If you've got an idea that needs a home—or hands to build what you've been dreaming about—fork the repo or stop by the Drop Yard. Just know: we do things the hard way, because the hard way is the right way.
